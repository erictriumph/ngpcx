How it works

Download the Scanner

A small Windows tool that reads your installed apps and drivers. No installation required — just run it.

2

Run the Scan

It scans your machine in seconds and sends the results to our server for analysis.

3

Get Your Score

See your ARM Readiness Score and a full breakdown of which apps are ready, which need emulation, and which won't work.

What we check
📦

Installed Apps

Every application on your machine, checked against our ARM compatibility database.

🔧

Drivers

Hardware drivers are often the hardest part of ARM migration — we flag the risky ones.

🏗️

Binary Architecture

We inspect the actual executable to confirm whether it's x86, x64, or already ARM64.

⚡

Emulation Impact

Apps running under emulation work but reduce battery life — we tell you which ones and by how much.

Understanding your score
✓ Native ARM64

Runs natively on Snapdragon. Full performance, full battery life.

⚡ Emulated

Works under x64 emulation. Slight performance and battery penalty.

✗ Unsupported

Will not run on ARM Windows. Needs a replacement or workaround.
