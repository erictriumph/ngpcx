Is Windows on ARM Right for You?

Windows on ARM laptops — built around the same energy-efficient processor design used in phones and tablets — can offer excellent battery life, quiet operation, and impressive power efficiency. They're becoming an increasingly compelling option for many Windows users.

But because the platform is still evolving, some Windows applications and devices work differently than others.

CheckMyARM scans the Windows PC you're using today and evaluates how your applications and devices are expected to work if you decide to make the move.

Compatibility information comes from multiple trusted sources and is continually improved by the CheckMyARM community.

Your results become a Workspace you can keep building on — mark what you've personally verified, flag what matters most, and save your progress to continue whenever you're ready.

For the best results, connect the devices you normally use before running a Light Assessment.

Emulation is a useful compatibility technology — many apps run very well under it. Native ARM64 apps may offer the best efficiency and battery life, and we show you which is which.

Understanding your score
